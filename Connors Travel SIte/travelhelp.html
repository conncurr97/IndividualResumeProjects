What is the true cost of international travel?

How much does this all cost you may ask? Well the answer is, it really all depends!
What it comes down to in the end is your travel style. Do you like to live large and stay
at the nicest hotels? Or are you ok with staying in dorm style hostels?
Are you ok with eating street food? Or do you want fine dining every night?
Everyone has their own travel style. Below are two example to get a rough idea
comparing both a budget and a more mid-range trip.

Budget Travel: 10 days Europe
Airfare Lodging Daily food cost Entertainment Nightlife
$400-$700 (Budget Airline off season) $6-10 a night (hostel) $15-25 a day (street food and grocery store) $10-30 a day $0-20 a night
Total Cost: $700-$1500
Mid-Range Travel: 10 days Europe
Airfare Lodging Daily food cost Entertainment Nightlife
$600-$1100 (standard airline high season) $40-150 a night (hotel) $35-70 a day (eating out decent meals) $25-75 a day $0-75 a night
Total Cost: $1600-$4450

Now You see! The cost of travel is way overblown!

People often have completely unrealitic expecations about the cost of travel.
Has you can see above, there is a way for nearly anyone to travel the world if they plan it out properly.
from the shoestring backpacker to the rich business person with expensive tastes. Whats your excuse?
