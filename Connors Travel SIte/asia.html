The Great Continent of Asia!


Japan:

This is the place that keeps bringing me back again and again.
Japan is where I fell in love with this great continent and I
am already planning on going back for a 3rd time in less than a 2 year
span.It's a completely wacky place to visit with the best food in the world,
in my opinion. Tokyo is my favorite city in the world by far I promise
you won't get bored. The Country is incredibly safe and a great entry into Asia for a beginner despite the language barrier.

Thailand:

Thailand, an entirely diffrent beast than Japan and a must see it it's own right. One of the coolest,
